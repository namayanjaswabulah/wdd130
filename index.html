Uganda

Uganda is a beautiful country located in East Africa, known for its diverse wildlife, rich culture, and friendly people. Often called the "Pearl of Africa," it is home to stunning landscapes including Lake Victoria, the source of the Nile River, and lush national parks like Queen Elizabeth and Murchison Falls. Uganda has a tropical climate, fertile soil, and a strong agricultural base. Its people are made up of many ethnic groups, each with unique traditions, languages, and customs that add to the nation's vibrant culture.
