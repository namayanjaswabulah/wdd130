Our History

Founded in 2005, White Water Rafting Adventures has safely guided thousands through the wild rivers of the Rocky Mountains. Our passion for the outdoors and commitment to safety has earned us a trusted reputation.

Adventure Awaits

We offer guided trips for all levels—from family floats to extreme whitewater. Join us for an unforgettable experience on Class III and IV rapids while taking in breathtaking scenery.

Meet the Team

Our certified guides bring years of experience and a passion for sharing nature. Each guide is trained in safety, river navigation, and hospitality to make your adventure both thrilling and safe.
